Hi, I’m Cameron. I’ve been pursuing a career in User Experience Design and Research for over 2 years. I have wide ranging experience working for clients in the private, non-profit, and government sectors. Much of my work involves designing technologies that make systems and services more equitable and accessible for the people using them. I have a background in documentary filmmaking, which has inspired many of the ways that I approach problems and communities in my design and research.

In my spare time I read, make music, cook, walk around my city, and run.
