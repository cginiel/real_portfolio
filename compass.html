Compass

A camping platform that empowers people and their friends to plan trips, track items, and discover new places.


Duration: Sep 2019 - Dec 2019 (Revisited 2021)
Team and role: Solo project
Type: Passion Project

Project process timeline

Problem

What is this campground like? What can I do while I'm there? How do I know what to bring with me?

Compass targets an issue that I'm personally passionate about. Planning a camping trip can be a complicated process. While people may get better at planning these trips the more they take, camping can be intimidating for the beginner. Plus, everyone can benefit from a more organized trip. Compass streamlines the trip planning process by allowing users to plan and coordinate trips with friends.
